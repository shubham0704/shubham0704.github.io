Research

My goal is to develop advanced computer vision systems that combine physical principles with modern deep learning approaches. Current research interests include:

  • 3D Vision and Scene Understanding
  • Physics-Informed Neural Networks
  • Generative Models for Visual Computing
  • Optimization Methods in Computer Vision
